  2. Safety Measures
  • Nose and mouth coverings are recommended to be used (a self-made or purchased mask, scarf, shawl, and other solutions are accepted)
  • Hygiene measures must be observed
  • Upon arrival in Greece from countries where the number of Covid-19 cases per 100,000 inhabitants exceeds 16 , public transport may only be used in order to get to the place of self-isolation, and a mouth and nose covering must be used.
  • Instructions available on public transport must be observed
