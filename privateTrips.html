Safety Measures

  2. Safety Measures
  • The need for the trip must be carefully considered
  • International passenger transport services are carried out through airports, ports, by bus, and by rail to and from countries with fewer than 25 new cases of Covid-19 infections per 100,000 inhabitants over the course of the last two weeks (the list of countries is available on the CDC website)
  • 14-day self-isolation must be observed after arriving in Greece from countries with more than 16 new cases of Covid-19 infection per 100,000 inhabitants over the course of the last two weeks (the list of countries is available on the CDC website)
  • 14-day self-isolation must be observed after arriving in Greece from a country to which international passenger transport services are not carried out
