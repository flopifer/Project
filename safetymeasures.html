Safety Measures

Using Public Transport Outdoor events Private Trips Shopping centres indoor Gatherings Sports activities
  • Instructions available on public transport must be observed,Nose and mouth coverings are recommended to be used (a self-made or purchased mask, scarf, shawl, and other solutions are accepted)
  • Hygiene measures must be observed
  • Upon arrival in Greece from countries where the number of Covid-19 cases per 100,000 inhabitants exceeds 16.
  • Public transport may only be used in order to get to the place of self-isolation, and a mouth and nose covering must be used.


  • Events for up to 1000 persons may be organised, subject to physical distancing and epidemiological safety measures
  • Outdoor drive-in cinemas and concerts, during which the visitors stay in their cars, may be organised (regardless of the number of participants)
  • Open-air cinemas must close no later than 2.00 pm
  • All owners or operators of playgrounds and outdoor gyms should consider how to put in place measures to support social distancing such as signs to remind users of the need for and adherence to social distancing in accordance with existing government guidance. In implementing measures, owners and operators should acknowledge that adults and children with certain conditions will find social distancing difficult.


  • The need for the trip must be carefully considered.
  • International passenger transport services are carried out through airports, ports, by bus, and by rail to and from countries with fewer than 25 new cases of Covid-19 infections per 100,000 inhabitants over the course of the last two weeks (the list of countries is available on the CDC website)
  • 14-day self-isolation must be observed after arriving in Greece from countries with more than 16 new cases of Covid-19 infection per 100,000 inhabitants over the course of the last two weeks (the list of countries is available on the CDC website)
  • 14-day self-isolation must be observed after arriving in Greece from a country to which international passenger transport services are not carried out.


  • Signs with instructions on physical distancing and current safety measures must be displayed The number of visitors must be limited at the location of sale
  • Use of mask optional.


  • Events for up to 100 persons may be organised in premises smaller than 1000 m2 and for up to 500 persons in premises larger than 1000 m2, subject to physical distancing, safety measures, and the rules set by the organiser


  • Outdoor sporting events can take place at any time of the day.
  • lasses and trainings for groups of up to 30 persons may be organised, subject to no overlap between different training groups.
  • Indoor sports competitions may be organised for up to 100 persons without spectators in premises smaller than 1000 m2; outdoor - for up to 1000 persons, including spectators.
  • Indoor sporting events may take place with spectators if the total indoor area is at least 1000 m2.
